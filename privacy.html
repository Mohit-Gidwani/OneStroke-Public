Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy describes how Mohit Gidwani ("we", "us") handles information when you use One Stroke (package: com.mohit.onestroke) on Google Play.

1. Information We Collect

Stored locally on your device only (never sent to us):
  • Level completion progress and star ratings
  • Coin balance and hint count
  • Selected skin / cosmetic preference
  • Sound and haptics on/off settings
  • Daily streak counter

All stored in Android SharedPreferences. We never transmit or access this data.

✓ No account required ✓ No email collected ✓ No location ✓ No camera/microphone ✓ No contacts

2. Third-Party Services & SDKs

Google AdMob — Interstitial & rewarded advertisements.
AdMob may collect: Android Advertising ID (GAID), IP address, device model, OS version, app usage signals for ad personalisation.

Google Privacy Policy  ·  AdMob Data Usage
Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — GDPR consent management for EEA/UK users. Your consent choice is stored locally and sent to Google's consent servers.

Google Privacy Policy

3. App Permissions

INTERNET — Required to load advertisements (AdMob) and process consent (UMP).
VIBRATE — Optional haptic feedback when drawing edges. Cannot be used to collect data.

4. Children's Privacy

One Stroke does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Ad content is controlled by Google AdMob.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

We collect no personal data ourselves. To manage data held by Google (AdMob):

  • Reset Advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID
  • Opt out: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Revoke consent: Re-open the App — UMP consent dialog appears at first launch

6. Data Retention

All local game data is retained until you uninstall the App or clear App data via Android Settings. We hold no server-side data.

7. Changes to This Policy

Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
